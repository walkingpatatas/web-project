ASSOCIATION OF COMPUTER STUDIES STUDENTS


The Association of Computer Studies Students is a recognized student organization of the University of the East - Caloocan Campus
that unites the computer studies students of the university. The ACSS aim to organize various events such as seminars, quiz bees,
e-sports competitions and outreach, serve as a bridge between the members and the rest of the university, create connections
with ICT professionals, and foster camaraderie within the organization.


Activities

Seminars

The ACSS organization holds seminars every semester.

Quizz Bee

They annually held a challenge game called quiz bee.

Games

All members also got to have fun by playing games.
MORE ABOUT ACSS

Officers

Without them, ACCS would not exist. They hold leadership roles in the everyday operations and management of the organization. They are the individuals who make the biggest decisions when it comes to the path of the organization.

Objectives

The ACSS Organization aims to provide ideas and information about the computer-related courses, subjects and possible career paths. This is aided with the development of skills and talents, not just academically but excel as well in extra-curricular.

events

Events are a great way to have fun with your other classmates and de-stress from the school works. The ACSS offer various events such as general assembly, webinars, games and e-sports, quiz bee, acts of giving and a lot more exciting stuffs.
