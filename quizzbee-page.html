QUIZZ BEE

BRAINACSS
January 23, 2020 | 2 PM | EN Briefing Room
The ACSS held a quiz bee anually called BrainACSS.
The first ever quiz bee was held in year 2020, at the EN Briefing Room. This annual event aims to test the intellectual capabilities of students in different subjects such as science, mathematics, social science, current events, and general knowledge.
BRAINACSS 2.0
February 05, 2021 | 3 PM | via Google Meet & Kahoot
As we welcome the new normal, this doesn't stop the ACSS organization to organize the annual quiz bee, BrainACSS. The second quiz bee challenge was held online using the platforms, Google Meet and Kahoot.
BRAINACSS 3.0
November 12, 2021 | 3 PM | via Google Meet & Discord
Quiz bee encourage students' self awareness of progress and
self assessment. By taking quizzes, students get instant feedback on their responses. And this can help them identify areas they need to develop themselves and highlight progress for them to be proud of. Having frequent quizzes and tests motivates study and reduces procrastination.
